About Bihar

Bihar is a state in eastern India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and religious significance. The capital city of Bihar is Patna.

Culture

The culture of Bihar is a blend of various traditions and has been influenced by different religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, and Islam. Bhojpuri is the widely spoken language in the state. The folk dances, music, and festivals of Bihar are vibrant and colorful.

Tourism

Bihar has many tourist attractions. Some of the popular ones include:

  • Historical sites like Nalanda, Rajgir, and Vaishali
  • Bodh Gaya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the place where Gautam Buddha attained enlightenment
  • Sonepur Mela, one of the largest cattle fairs in Asia
  • Takht Sri Patna Sahib, a holy place for Sikhs
  • Kakolat Waterfall, a scenic waterfall in Nawada district

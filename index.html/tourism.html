Tourism of Bihar

Explore the Rich Heritage

Bihar is home to several ancient and significant sites that showcase the rich cultural and historical heritage of India. From the ancient city of Patna to the Buddhist pilgrimage site of Bodh Gaya, Bihar offers a treasure trove of experiences for history enthusiasts.

Natural Beauty at Its Best

Bihar boasts of picturesque landscapes, serene rivers, and lush greenery. The state is blessed with beautiful attractions such as the Vikramshila Gangetic Dolphin Sanctuary, Valmiki National Park, and the mesmerizing waterfalls of Kakolat and Karkat.

Discover Spiritual Bliss

Bihar is the birthplace of Buddhism, and it holds great significance for followers of the religion. Explore the renowned Mahabodhi Temple Complex in Bodh Gaya, the Nalanda University ruins, and the ancient rock-cut caves of Barabar Hills.
